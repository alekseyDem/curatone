Expert jury board

Become an International Jury Member

Validate your expertise, influence the art world, and build a world-class professional portfolio.

Applications
Reviewed individually by the Curatone.art committee. No payment is requested until your application has been approved.

Curatone.art invites established artists, curators, and industry professionals to join our prestigious jury panel. Serving as a juror for an international competition is not only a contribution to the arts — it is a significant milestone that confirms your status as an expert in the global creative community.

Fair selection & quality standards

MANDATORY PRE-SELECTION

To maintain the integrity of our exhibitions, all potential jurors undergo a mandatory pre-selection process. Qualification requirements are high and identical for all applicants, regardless of the package chosen.

Approval first
We review every CV and portfolio individually. No payment is requested until your application has been officially approved by the committee.
Professional integrity
Selection is based solely on your achievements and expertise — never on the package you choose.

Qualification criteria

Appointment to the jury board is based on several key pillars.

01
Extensive experience in the visual arts, design, or curatorial practice — typically 10+ years.
02
Prestigious awards (Red Dot, IDA, or equivalent) and membership in recognized professional associations (IAA/AIAP, NAWA).
03
Advanced degrees in Fine Arts or related disciplines, and a history of pedagogical or research activity.

Merit & peer recognition

The selection process

STAGE 01
Peer review
Current jury members — established experts and award winners — review the candidate's portfolio and cast votes.
STAGE 02
Majority vote
A candidate must receive a majority of positive votes from this peer group to be considered.
STAGE 03
Final decision
The Project Director reviews the board's recommendation and makes the final appointment.
The status of a judge is earned through excellence. Any administrative fees involved are strictly for the technical preparation of legal reports and documentation.

How to apply

Send an email to info@curatone.art with the subject line "Jury Application — [Your Full Name]". Your email must include:

01
Professional bio & statement
A brief summary of your career and artistic philosophy.
02
Portfolio / website
A link to your personal website or professional social media profiles.
03
Background highlights
A summary of your exhibitions, publications, awards, or association memberships.
04
Preferred tier
State your chosen option: Regular or Professional.
Email your application
Application review
All applications are reviewed by the committee. The decision to approve or decline is final. Once approved, you'll receive instructions to select your package and complete enrollment.
Judge again
Email info@curatone.art with subject "I Want to Judge". Additional judging opportunities may require an extra fee depending on the competition.
