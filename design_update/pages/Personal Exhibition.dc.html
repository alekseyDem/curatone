Send an email to info@curatone.art with the subject line "Solo Exhibition Application — [Your Full Name]". Include in your message:

Artist statementA brief statement of intent

Conditions
All applications are reviewed by our curatorial team.
Selected artists are contacted within 14 business days.
A $65 exhibition fee confirms participation — covering technical setup, publication, and promotional support.
The exhibition stays online for a minimum of 3 months; extensions and updates on request.

Good to know

You keep 100% of sales
We take no commission from artwork sales. Buyers are directed straight to your contact or shop.
Promotional use
By applying, you agree to our Terms & Conditions regarding the use of your artworks for promotional purposes.
Yours to update
Request an extension or refresh your exhibition at any time by contacting us.
