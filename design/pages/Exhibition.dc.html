Personal exhibition · June 10 — August 10, 2026

Meryem Berrada: Selected Works

Twelve works from 2022 – 2026 by the winner of Moments of Life 2025. Curated by Curatone.art; presented with a published interview and full exhibition documentation.
Read the interview Artist profile

About the exhibition

Selected Works brings together twelve paintings and mixed-media pieces made between 2022 and 2026, tracing the development of Berrada's practice from early watercolor studies to the layered panel works that earned her the top award at Moments of Life 2025. The exhibition is organized in three loose movements — studies of place, the weaving cycle, and recent works on memory — presented here in a single continuous sequence.

Berrada works from Casablanca, and the city's architecture, textile markets, and coastal light recur throughout the selection. Rather than documenting these subjects, the works treat them as remembered structures: simplified, re-colored, and rebuilt from pattern. The curators have paired early and recent treatments of the same motifs so that this process of revision is visible across the room.

The exhibition is accompanied by a published interview with the artist and full documentation: each work is catalogued with title, year, medium, and dimensions, and the exhibition itself is recorded in the platform's permanent archive.

From the interview
