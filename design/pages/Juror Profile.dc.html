Josephine Florens

Professional oil painter · Germany

Josephine Florens is a professional oil painter based in Germany, working primarily in marine and landscape painting. Her practice combines classical technique with contemporary subject matter, and her work has been shown in juried exhibitions across Europe.

She serves on the Curatone jury across painting and drawing categories, scoring each assigned work independently under the platform's published criteria.
