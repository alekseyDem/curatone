Submission guidelines

Curatone Art & Research Journal (ISSN 3054-6621) publishes original research on contemporary art and design. All submissions undergo double-blind peer review; accepted articles are published open access with a registered DOI.

1. Scope

The journal accepts contributions on contemporary art and design practice, curation, art theory, materiality, and the professional structures of the art world. Contributions to the ongoing research initiative — The Impact of Contemporary Art and Design on Global Social Structures — are accepted on a rolling basis.

2. Article types

TypeDescriptionLength
Research articleOriginal empirical or theoretical research5,000–9,000 words
ReviewCritical synthesis of existing scholarship4,000–7,000 words
Practice reportDocumented account of artistic or curatorial practice3,000–5,000 words

3. Formatting

3.1Manuscripts in PDF or DOCX; 12 pt body text, double-spaced, pages numbered.
3.2Citations and references in APA 7 style.
3.3Abstract of up to 250 words and 4–6 keywords included in the manuscript.
3.4Figures embedded for review; source files (min 300 dpi) supplied separately upon acceptance, with captions and credits.
3.5The review copy must be anonymized: no author names, affiliations, or self-identifying references.

4. Review process

STEP 01
Editorial screening
Within 2 weeks
STEP 02
Double-blind review
6–10 weeks · two reviewers
STEP 03
Decision & revisions
Accept · revise · decline
STEP 04
Publication
Next quarterly issue · DOI

5. Publication terms

Accepted articles are published open access; authors retain copyright under the journal's license. Every article receives a registered DOI and is permanently archived. There are no reader fees. Authors receive a publication record suitable for professional documentation.
