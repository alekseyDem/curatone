About the artist

Ivelina Dagsvold is a Bulgarian painter working in acrylic and mixed media. Her practice draws on textile traditions of the Balkans, translating woven pattern into layered painterly structures. She holds an MA in Fine Arts from the National Academy of Art, Sofia.

Her work has been exhibited in group shows in Sofia, Vienna, and Berlin, and is held in private collections across Europe. She is a member of the Union of Bulgarian Artists and a recipient of awards in international juried competitions, including a Gold award at Connections 2026.
