About the work

Threads of Home examines connection through the material language of textile patterns translated into paint. Built from layered acrylic passages, the work draws on the artist's family archive of woven fabrics, treating pattern as a record of migration and belonging. The jury noted the disciplined color structure and the coherence between concept and technique.
